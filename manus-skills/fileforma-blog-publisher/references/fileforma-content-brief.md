# FileForma Content Brief

FileForma is a free online file conversion website focused on image and PDF tools.

Primary goals:

- Earn organic search traffic.
- Build trust for free file tools.
- Keep content practical rather than promotional.
- Support future AdSense monetization without thin or misleading pages.

Audience:

- Students, office workers, small businesses, creators, admins, and everyday users.
- Global English search audience.
- Users often need a quick answer before uploading, emailing, or submitting a file.

Content pillars:

- PDF workflows
- Image format decisions
- Office productivity
- Data privacy and file safety

Writing style:

- Clear, direct, and helpful.
- Explain tradeoffs.
- Avoid fake precision and unsupported claims.
- Do not overpromise perfect conversion for complex PDFs, OCR, or layout recovery.
